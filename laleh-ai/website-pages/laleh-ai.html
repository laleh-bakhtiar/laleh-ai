Introducing Laleh AI

A Custom GPT Experience Celebrating
the late Dr. Laleh Bakhtiar’s Legacy

Try Laleh AI on ChatGPT
Introducing Laleh AI: a distinct GPT platform, currently in beta testing, designed to commemorate Dr. Laleh Bakhtiar. This AI experience, in its development phase, is enriched with Dr. Bakhtiar’s writings, offering a unique perspective on spirituality, ethics, and social justice.

Sample Prompts for Users:
Users can inquire about Dr. Bakhtiar’s Quran interpretation, views on gender equality, the blending of her Iranian heritage with American upbringing, and her insights on the role of women in Islamic history.

Sample Prompts for Users:
  • “Can you tell me about Dr. Laleh Bakhtiar’s interpretation of verse 4:34 in the Quran?”
  • “What are Dr. Bakhtiar's views on gender equality in Islamic teachings?”
  • “How did Dr. Bakhtiar blend her Iranian heritage with her American upbringing in her work?”
  • “Can you share insights from Dr. Bakhtiar on the role of women in Islamic history?”
  • “Please provide a motivational message based on Dr. Bakhtiar’s teachings for Ramadan.”
  • How did the book “Little Women” inspire Bakhtiar in her youth?

“Laleh AI encapsulates my mother’s journey. This beta version is a technological innovation and a heartfelt homage,” states Davar Ardalan, Dr. Bakhtiar’s daughter. The beta version of Laleh AI, crafted by Davar Ardalan at TulipAI and in collaboration with the Institute of Traditional Psychoethics and Guidance, marks a significant step in digitizing Dr. Bakhtiar’s work.

⚠️ Disclaimer: Laleh AI is an innovative, evolving experience, blending scholarly insights with AI technology. We are dedicated to accuracy but also recognize the project’s beta nature.
